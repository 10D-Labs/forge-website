# Delete Your Account

**Last Updated:** January 2026

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Forge account and all associated data, you have two options:

---

## Option 1: Delete Through the App

1. Open the Forge app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

Your account and data will be deleted immediately.

---

## Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

**Email:** [support@forgetrainer.ai](mailto:support@forgetrainer.ai?subject=Delete%20My%20Account)

**Subject:** Delete My Account

**Include:** The email address associated with your Forge account

Note that for security concerns the email **must** be sent from the email associated with the account.

We will process your request within 30 days.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Profile information (name, email, date of birth, biological sex)
- Body measurements and weight history
- Workout history and logged sets
- Personal records
- AI chat history
- Training preferences and goals
- Profile picture

---
