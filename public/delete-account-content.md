# Delete Your Account

**Last Updated:** February 2026

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Forge account and all associated data, you have two options:

---

## Option 1: Delete Through the App

1. Open the Forge app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm deletion

Your account and data will be deleted immediately.

---

## Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

**Email:** [support@forgetrainer.ai](mailto:support@forgetrainer.ai?subject=Delete%20My%20Account)

**Subject:** Delete My Account

**Include:** The email address associated with your Forge account

Note that for security concerns the email **must** be sent from the email associated with the account.

We will process your request within 30 days.

---

## Important: Cancel Your Subscription First

Deleting your account does **not** automatically cancel your subscription. You must separately cancel your subscription through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings before or after deleting your account to avoid further charges.

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Profile information (name, email, date of birth, biological sex)
- Body measurements and weight history
- Workout plans and training programs
- AI conversation history and chat messages
- AI coaching profiles, memories, and knowledge graph data
- Personal records
- Profile picture and any submitted feedback screenshots
- Training preferences and goals

---

## What May Be Retained

Some data is retained for legal, compliance, or technical reasons:

- **Anonymized workout and usage data**: Workout session records and AI usage logs are anonymized (your user identifier is removed) and may be retained for product analytics and service improvement. This data cannot be linked back to you.
- **Third-party service records**: Some data already transmitted to third-party service providers (such as subscription management and analytics providers) may persist in those systems according to their own retention policies. We make reasonable efforts to propagate deletion requests where technically feasible.
