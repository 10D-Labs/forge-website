## Starting Your Fitness Journey

Getting started is the hardest part. The good news: you don't need to know everything. You just need to start with the basics and build from there.

**Key principles:**
- Guidance dramatically improves results for beginners
- Consistency matters more than perfection
- Start simple and add complexity over time
- Having a plan eliminates guesswork and builds confidence

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a personal trainer as a beginner?
Not necessarily, but having guidance dramatically improves results and prevents injury. AI trainers like Forge provide personalized guidance at a fraction of traditional trainer costs.

### How often should I work out as a beginner?
Start with 3 days per week. This gives your body time to recover while building the habit. You can increase frequency as you adapt.

### What should my first workout look like?
Focus on learning basic movement patterns: squat, hinge, push, pull. Start with lighter weights to master technique before adding intensity.

### How long before I see results?
With consistent training, you'll feel stronger within 2-3 weeks. Visible changes typically start around 6-8 weeks. Long-term transformation takes 3-6 months.
