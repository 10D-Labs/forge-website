## The Foundation of Strength Training

Effective strength training comes down to a few core principles that, when applied consistently, produce reliable results. These fundamentals work whether you're a beginner or experienced lifter.

**Key principles:**
- Progressive overload is the primary driver of muscle growth
- Proper workout splits optimize recovery and training frequency
- Compound movements build the most strength per time invested
- Consistency beats intensity over the long term
