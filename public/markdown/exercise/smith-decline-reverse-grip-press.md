# Smith Decline Reverse Grip Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The smith decline reverse grip press is a chest exercise performed on a smith machine in a decline position, using a reverse (underhand) grip to target the lower pectorals and engage the tricep and shoulders.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Chest
- **Equipment:** Smith Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Chest
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Smith Decline Reverse Grip Press

1. Adjust the smith machine to a decline position.
2. Lie down on the bench with your feet secured under the foot pads.
3. Grasp the barbell with a reverse grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
4. Unrack the barbell and lower it towards your chest, keeping your elbows tucked in.
5. Pause for a moment at the bottom, then push the barbell back up to the starting position.
6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
