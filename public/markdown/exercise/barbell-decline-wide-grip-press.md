# Barbell Decline Wide Grip Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The barbell decline wide grip press is a compound chest exercise performed on a decline bench using a barbell with a wide grip. It targets the lower portion of the pectoral muscles and also engages the tricep and shoulders.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Chest
- **Equipment:** Barbell
- **Target Muscle:** Chest
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Barbell Decline Wide Grip Press

1. Lie on a decline bench with your feet secured and your head lower than your hips.
2. Grasp the barbell with a wide grip, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
3. Lower the barbell to your chest, keeping your elbows out to the sides.
4. Push the barbell back up to the starting position, fully extending your arms.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
