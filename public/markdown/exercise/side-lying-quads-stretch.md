# Side Lying Quads Stretch - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

A static stretch performed lying on your side to target the quadriceps, often used to improve flexibility and reduce muscle tightness.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Quads
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Stretching

---

## How to Perform Side Lying Quads Stretch

1. Lie on your side with your legs straight.
2. Bend your top leg and grab your ankle or foot with your hand.
3. Gently pull your ankle or foot towards your glutes until you feel a stretch in your quads.
4. Hold the stretch for 20-30 seconds.
5. Release the stretch and repeat on the other side.
