# Dumbbell Pronated Grip Tricep Extension - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell pronate-grip tricep extension is an upper arm exercise that targets the tricep using a dumbbell with a pronated (palms-down) grip. It is performed seated, with the dumbbell extended overhead and lowered behind the head.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Forearms
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Pronated Grip Tricep Extension

1. Sit on a bench or chair with your back straight and feet flat on the ground.
2. Hold a dumbbell with both hands, palms facing down, and extend your arms straight up overhead.
3. Keeping your upper arms close to your head and elbows pointing forward, slowly lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbows.
4. Pause for a moment, then extend your arms back up to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
