# Dumbbell Decline Single Arm Hammer Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell decline one arm hammer press is a unilateral pressing movement performed on a decline bench, targeting the tricep with secondary emphasis on the chest and shoulders. It requires core stability and coordination due to the decline angle and single arm execution.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Chest, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Decline Single Arm Hammer Press

1. Lie on a decline bench with a dumbbell in one hand, resting on your chest.
2. Extend your arm straight up, keeping your elbow slightly bent.
3. Lower the dumbbell down towards your shoulder, keeping your elbow close to your body.
4. Press the dumbbell back up to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch arms.
