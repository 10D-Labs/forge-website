# Pushup on Lower Arms

## Quick answer

The pushup on lower arms is a bodyweight exercise targeting the tricep, performed from a plank position on the forearms. It also engages the chest and shoulders. Intermediate arms exercise using body weight. Targets triceps, also works chest, shoulders.

- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Chest, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Start in a plank position with your forearms on the ground and elbows directly below your shoulders.
2. Engage your core and keep your body in a straight line from head to toe.
3. Lower your chest towards the ground by bending your elbows, keeping them close to your body.
4. Pause for a moment at the bottom, then push yourself back up to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Start from a forearm plank and press up one arm at a time, leading with the same arm to avoid imbalances.
- Keep your hips level and avoid rocking side to side by bracing your core throughout the entire movement.
- Alternate which arm leads each rep or each set to develop both sides evenly.

## Common mistakes

- Allowing the hips to sag or pike up during the transition between forearms and hands.
- Placing the hands too far forward when pressing up, which reduces tricep engagement and puts extra stress on the shoulders.
- Rocking the body from side to side excessively, which indicates the core isn't engaged tightly enough.

## FAQ

### What is a Pushup on Lower Arms also called?

This exercise is commonly known as a commando Pushup or up-down plank. It combines a forearm plank with a Pushup to challenge both the triceps and core.

### How do I prevent wrist pain during Pushups on lower arms?

Spread your fingers wide and press through the entire palm when in the hands-up position. If pain persists, try performing the exercise on Pushup handles or fists.

### Is this exercise good for building triceps?

Yes, the pressing-up phase heavily loads the triceps since you're pushing your entire bodyweight from a forearm position. It also builds significant core stability.

### What muscles does the Pushup on Lower Arms work?

The Pushup on Lower Arms primarily targets the triceps, with secondary activation in the chest, shoulders. It's classified as a strength exercise for the arms.

### Is the Pushup on Lower Arms good for beginners?

The Pushup on Lower Arms is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of triceps strength with easier variations first.
