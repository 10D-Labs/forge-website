# Barbell Seated Good Morning - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The barbell seated good morning is a strength exercise that targets the glutes, with secondary emphasis on the hamstrings and lower back. It involves sitting on a bench with a barbell on your upper back, hinging forward at the hips, and returning to the upright position.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Barbell
- **Target Muscle:** Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Lower Back
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Barbell Seated Good Morning

1. Sit on a bench with your feet flat on the ground and a barbell resting on your upper back.
2. Keep your back straight and your chest up.
3. Slowly hinge forward at the hips, lowering your torso towards the ground.
4. Pause for a moment at the bottom, then push through your heels to return to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
