# Lying Squat

## Quick answer

The lying squat is a lower body exercise performed on a leg press machine, targeting the glutes as the primary muscle group. It also engages the quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves. The movement involves lying on your back, pushing The upward with your legs, and then lowering it back down in a controlled manner. Beginner legs exercise using machine. Targets glutes, also works quadriceps, hamstrings, calves.

- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Glutes
- **Secondary Muscles:** Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Calves
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Adjust the leg press machine to a comfortable weight.
2. Lie on your back on the leg press machine with your feet on the footplate.
3. Position your feet shoulder-width apart and slightly angled outwards.
4. Grip the handles of the leg press machine for stability.
5. Engage your glutes and core muscles.
6. Push through your heels and extend your legs to lift the sled.
7. Lower The back down by bending your knees and hips.
8. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Press through the full foot with emphasis on the heels to target the glutes and hamstrings.
- Position your feet at shoulder width in the center of the footplate for balanced muscle recruitment.
- Control the descent and don't let the sled slam down at the bottom of each rep.

## Common mistakes

- Letting the lower back round off the pad by lowering the sled too far.
- Pushing through the toes, which shifts load to the quads and increases knee stress.
- Locking out the knees at the top, especially with heavy weight.

## FAQ

### What's the difference between a Lying Squat and a regular Leg Press?

They're very similar. The Lying Squat (horizontal Leg Press) has you lying flat, while some Leg Presses are at a 45-degree angle. The lying version can feel more comfortable for the lower back.

### Is the Lying Squat good for beginners?

Yes. The machine controls the movement path, which makes it safer than free weight squats for learning lower body pushing mechanics.

### How much weight should I use on the Lying Squat?

Start with just the sled and add weight gradually. Most people can handle more weight on a Leg Press than a barbell squat because the back is fully supported.

### What muscles does the Lying Squat work?

The Lying Squat primarily targets the glutes, with secondary activation in the quadriceps, hamstrings, calves. It's classified as a strength exercise for the legs.

### Is the Lying Squat good for beginners?

Yes, the Lying Squat is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building glutes strength.
