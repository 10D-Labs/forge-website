# Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl

## Quick answer

The band one arm overhead bicep curl is an isolation exercise targeting the bicep, performed with a resistance band. It involves curling the arm overhead while keeping the upper arm stationary, providing resistance throughout the movement. Beginner arms exercise using resistance band. Targets biceps, also works forearms, shoulders.

- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Biceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Forearms, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and place one end of the band under your foot.
2. Hold the other end of the band with your arm fully extended overhead, palm facing forward.
3. Keeping your upper arm stationary, curl your forearm towards your shoulder, squeezing your biceps.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your forearm back to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch arms.

## Pro tips

- Keep your upper arm vertical and close to your ear throughout the movement.
- Curl by bending the elbow and bringing your hand behind your head, then extend back to the start.
- Use a light band because the overhead position makes this movement extremely challenging for the bicep.

## Common mistakes

- Letting the elbow drift forward or outward instead of keeping the upper arm vertical and stationary.
- Arching the lower back to compensate for the overhead position, which strains the lumbar spine.
- Using too thick a band, which prevents a full range of motion in the overhead position.

## FAQ

### Why do Bicep Curls overhead?

The overhead position places the long head of the biceps in a fully stretched position, which maximizes the stretch stimulus. This is excellent for targeting the bicep peak.

### Is this exercise safe for shoulders?

It requires good shoulder mobility. If you cannot comfortably hold your arm overhead with the elbow by your ear, work on shoulder mobility first.

### How many reps should you do?

12-15 reps per arm with a light band. The overhead position limits the weight you can use, so higher reps with controlled form are best.

### What muscles does the Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl work?

The Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl primarily targets the biceps, with secondary activation in the forearms, shoulders. It's classified as a strength exercise for the arms.

### Is the Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl good for beginners?

Yes, the Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building biceps strength.
