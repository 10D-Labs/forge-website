# Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The band one arm overhead bicep curl is an isolation exercise targeting the bicep, performed with a resistance band. It involves curling the arm overhead while keeping the upper arm stationary, providing resistance throughout the movement.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Biceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Forearms, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Band Single Arm Overhead Bicep Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and place one end of the band under your foot.
2. Hold the other end of the band with your arm fully extended overhead, palm facing forward.
3. Keeping your upper arm stationary, curl your forearm towards your shoulder, squeezing your biceps.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your forearm back to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch arms.
