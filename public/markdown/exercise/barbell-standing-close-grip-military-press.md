# Barbell Standing Close Grip Military Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The barbell standing close grip military press is a compound strength exercise targeting the deltoids, with secondary emphasis on the tricep and upper back. It is performed by pressing a barbell overhead from shoulder height with a close grip, requiring core stability and shoulder strength.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Barbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Upper Back
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Barbell Standing Close Grip Military Press

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold the barbell with an overhand grip, hands slightly closer than shoulder-width apart.
2. Lift the barbell to shoulder height, keeping your elbows close to your body.
3. Press the barbell overhead, extending your arms fully.
4. Lower the barbell back to shoulder height.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
