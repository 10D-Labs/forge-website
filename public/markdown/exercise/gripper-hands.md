# Gripper Hands - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The gripper hands exercise targets the forearms using a machine. It involves gripping and squeezing the handles to strengthen the forearm muscles, with secondary activation of the bicep and tricep.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Forearms
- **Secondary Muscles:** Biceps, Triceps
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Gripper Hands

1. Adjust the seat height and grip the handles of the machine.
2. Keep your back straight and your feet flat on the ground.
3. Exhale and squeeze the handles, contracting your forearms.
4. Hold the contraction for a second, then slowly release and return to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
