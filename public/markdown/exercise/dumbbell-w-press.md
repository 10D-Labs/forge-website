# Dumbbell W Press

## Quick answer

The dumbbell W-press is a shoulder exercise performed with dumbbells, focusing on the deltoid muscles. It involves pressing the weights overhead from a 'W' position, engaging the shoulders, tricep, and chest. Beginner shoulders exercise using dumbbell. Targets shoulders, also works triceps, chest.

- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Chest
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Sit on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward.
2. Raise the dumbbells to shoulder height, elbows bent and palms facing forward.
3. Press the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended overhead.
4. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Start with your arms in a W shape (elbows bent at roughly 90 degrees, forearms angled outward) before pressing overhead.
- Rotate your wrists slightly as you press so your palms face forward at the top, engaging all three deltoid heads through the range of motion.
- Squeeze your shoulder blades down and back at the starting position to protect the shoulder joint.

## Common mistakes

- Starting with the elbows too low or too far forward, losing the W position that gives this press its unique deltoid activation.
- Arching the lower back excessively at the top of the press instead of bracing the core to keep the torso upright.

## FAQ

### What's the difference between a W press and a regular shoulder press?

The W press starts with your arms in a W shape (elbows bent, slightly abducted) and includes a slight rotation during the press. This engages the rotator cuff and rear deltoids more than a standard shoulder press.

### Is the W press good for shoulder rehab?

Yes, when done with light weights it can strengthen the rotator cuff and improve scapular stability. However, consult a physical therapist before using it for rehabilitation.

### What muscles does the Dumbbell W Press work?

The Dumbbell W Press primarily targets the shoulders, with secondary activation in the triceps, chest. It's classified as a strength exercise for the shoulders.

### Is the Dumbbell W Press good for beginners?

Yes, the Dumbbell W Press is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building shoulders strength.
