# Dumbbell W Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell W-press is a shoulder exercise performed with dumbbells, focusing on the deltoid muscles. It involves pressing the weights overhead from a 'W' position, engaging the shoulders, tricep, and chest.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Chest
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell W Press

1. Sit on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward.
2. Raise the dumbbells to shoulder height, elbows bent and palms facing forward.
3. Press the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended overhead.
4. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
