# Barbell Standing Overhead Tricep Extension - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The barbell standing overhead tricep extension is a strength exercise targeting the tricep, performed by holding a barbell overhead and lowering it behind the head before extending the arms to the starting position.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Barbell
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Barbell Standing Overhead Tricep Extension

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell with an overhand grip.
2. Raise the barbell overhead, fully extending your arms.
3. Keeping your upper arms close to your head, slowly lower the barbell behind your head by bending your elbows.
4. Pause for a moment, then raise the barbell back to the starting position by extending your arms.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
