# Cable Single Arm Kneeling High Row - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The cable one arm kneeling high row is an exercise that targets the upper back, performed using a cable machine. It also engages the bicep and shoulders, and is done in a kneeling position to help isolate the upper back muscles.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Back
- **Equipment:** Cable
- **Target Muscle:** Upper Back
- **Secondary Muscles:** Biceps, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Cable Single Arm Kneeling High Row

1. Attach a handle to a cable machine at waist height.
2. Kneel down facing the cable machine and grab the handle with one hand.
3. Keep your back straight and your core engaged.
4. Pull the handle towards your chest, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
5. Pause for a moment at the top of the movement.
6. Slowly release the handle back to the starting position.
7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
8. Switch sides and repeat the exercise with the other arm.
