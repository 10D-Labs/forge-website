# Hack Calf Raise - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The hack calf raise is a lower leg exercise performed on a leg press machine, primarily targeting the calves. It involves raising and lowering the heels while standing on the platform, focusing on calf muscle contraction.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Calves
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Hack Calf Raise

1. Adjust the leg press machine to a comfortable weight.
2. Stand on the leg press machine with your toes on the platform and your heels hanging off.
3. Hold onto the handles for stability.
4. Raise your heels as high as possible by pushing through the balls of your feet.
5. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your heels back down to the starting position.
6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
