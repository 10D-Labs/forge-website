# Dumbbell Standing Alternate Overhead Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell standing alternate overhead press is a shoulder exercise that involves pressing dumbbells overhead one arm at a time while standing. This movement targets the deltoid muscles and also engages the tricep and core for stability.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Core
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Standing Alternate Overhead Press

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder level with your palms facing forward.
2. Press one dumbbell overhead, fully extending your arm.
3. Lower the dumbbell back to shoulder level.
4. Repeat with the other arm.
5. Continue alternating arms for the desired number of repetitions.
