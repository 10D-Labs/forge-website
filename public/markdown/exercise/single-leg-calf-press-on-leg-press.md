# Single Leg Calf Press on Leg Press

## Quick answer

The one leg calf press on leg press is a machine-based exercise targeting the calves, performed one leg at a time for increased isolation and intensity. It also engages the hamstrings and glutes as secondary muscles. Intermediate legs exercise using machine. Targets calves, also works hamstrings, glutes.

- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Calves
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Adjust the seat of the leg press machine so that your knees are slightly bent when your feet are on the sled.
2. Sit on the machine with your back against the backrest and your feet on the sled, shoulder-width apart.
3. Place your toes and the balls of your feet on the sled, keeping your heels off.
4. Push The forward by extending your ankles, keeping your knees slightly bent.
5. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower The back down by flexing your ankles.
6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Center one foot on the bottom edge of the sled with the heel completely off the platform.
- Use about half the weight you would for bilateral Calf Presses and focus on a full, slow range of motion.
- Press through the big toe side of the foot to prevent the ankle from rolling outward under load.

## Common mistakes

- Using the same weight as the bilateral version, leading to cheating and reduced range of motion.
- Letting the foot slide down the sled during the set because the ball of the foot is not firmly positioned.
- Rushing through reps without pausing at either the stretch or the contraction.

## FAQ

### Why do single-leg Calf Presses?

To identify and correct calf strength imbalances between legs and to increase the intensity per calf without needing to load more total weight on the machine.

### Is this safer than single-leg Standing Calf Raises with a barbell?

Yes. The Leg Press machine fully supports your body, eliminating balance concerns and spinal loading, making it a safer way to train single-leg calf strength.

### How do I know which leg is weaker?

Test each leg separately with the same weight. The weaker leg will fatigue sooner, have less range of motion, or feel more unstable during the pressing motion.

### What muscles does the Single Leg Calf Press on Leg Press work?

The Single Leg Calf Press on Leg Press primarily targets the calves, with secondary activation in the hamstrings, glutes. It's classified as a strength exercise for the legs.

### Is the Single Leg Calf Press on Leg Press good for beginners?

The Single Leg Calf Press on Leg Press is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of calves strength with easier variations first.
