# Side Lying Adductor Stretch - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The assisted side lying adductor stretch is a flexibility exercise targeting the inner thigh muscles (adductors), performed while lying on your side and using a bench or step for assistance.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Machine
- **Target Muscle:** Adductors
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Stretching

---

## How to Perform Side Lying Adductor Stretch

1. Lie on your side with your legs straight and stacked on top of each other.
2. Bend your bottom leg slightly for stability.
3. Place your top foot on a stable surface, such as a bench or step.
4. Keeping your top leg straight, slowly lower it towards the ground, feeling a stretch in your inner thigh.
5. Hold the stretch for 20-30 seconds.
6. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.
