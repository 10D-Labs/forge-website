# Two Toe Touch

## Quick answer

The two toe touch is a bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the spine, with secondary emphasis on the hamstrings and glutes. It involves bending forward at the waist to touch your toes, promoting flexibility and mobility in the posterior chain. Beginner back exercise using body weight. Targets lower back, also works hamstrings, glutes.

- **Body Part:** Back
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Lower Back
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Mobility

---

## How to perform

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms extended out to the sides.
2. Bend forward at the waist, keeping your back straight and your knees slightly bent.
3. Reach down towards your toes with both hands, keeping your legs straight.
4. Pause for a moment at the bottom, then slowly return to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Reach toward your toes with both hands simultaneously while keeping your arms extended.
- Hinge from the hips with a flat back rather than rounding your spine to touch the floor.
- Hold the bottom position for 2-3 seconds to get a meaningful stretch in the hamstrings and lower back.

## Common mistakes

- Rounding the entire spine to reach the floor, which compresses the spinal discs and reduces the Hamstring Stretch.
- Bouncing at the bottom to reach further, which can cause muscle strains.
- Locking the knees aggressively, which stresses the back of the knee joint.

## FAQ

### What does the Two Toe Touch stretch?

It stretches the hamstrings, lower back, and calves. It also provides a gentle stretch for the entire posterior chain.

### Is the Two Toe Touch good for lower back mobility?

Yes, performed gently with a hip hinge, it improves lower back and hamstring flexibility. Avoid forcing the stretch if you feel sharp pain.

### Should I do toe touches every day?

Daily toe touch stretching is safe and beneficial for most people. Consistent practice is the fastest way to improve hamstring and lower back flexibility.

### What muscles does the Two Toe Touch work?

The Two Toe Touch primarily targets the lower back, with secondary activation in the hamstrings, glutes. It's classified as a mobility exercise for the back.

### Is the Two Toe Touch good for beginners?

Yes, the Two Toe Touch is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building lower back strength.
