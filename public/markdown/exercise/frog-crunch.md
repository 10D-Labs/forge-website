# Frog Crunch - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The frog crunch is a bodyweight abdominal exercise that targets the abs and also engages the hip flexors. It is performed lying on your back, curling your torso up while keeping your feet together and knees bent.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Abs
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hip Flexors
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Frog Crunch

1. Lie flat on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
2. Place your hands behind your head with your elbows pointing outwards.
3. Engaging your abs, lift your upper body off the ground, curling forward until your torso is at a 45 degree angle.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your upper body back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
