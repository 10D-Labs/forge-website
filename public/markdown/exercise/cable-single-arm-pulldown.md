# Cable Single Arm Pulldown - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The cable one arm pulldown is a unilateral exercise that targets the latissimus dorsi using a cable machine. It also engages the bicep and forearms as secondary muscles. The movement requires good form and control to isolate the lats and avoid compensatory movements.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Back
- **Equipment:** Cable
- **Target Muscle:** Lats
- **Secondary Muscles:** Biceps, Forearms
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Cable Single Arm Pulldown

1. Attach a single handle to a high pulley cable machine.
2. Stand facing the machine with your feet shoulder-width apart.
3. Grasp the handle with an overhand grip and extend your arm fully.
4. Keep your back straight and your core engaged.
5. Pull the handle down towards your side while keeping your elbow close to your body.
6. Pause for a moment at the bottom of the movement, squeezing your lat muscle.
7. Slowly release the handle back to the starting position.
8. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
9. Switch sides and repeat the exercise with the other arm.
