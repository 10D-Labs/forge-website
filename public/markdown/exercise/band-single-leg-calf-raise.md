# Band Single Leg Calf Raise

## Quick answer

The band single leg calf raise is a lower leg exercise that targets the calves using a resistance band. It also engages the ankles and feet, helping to improve strength and stability in the lower leg. The movement is performed one leg at a time, which increases the balance and coordination required. Intermediate legs exercise using resistance band. Targets calves, also works ankles, feet.

- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Calves
- **Secondary Muscles:** Ankles, Feet
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Stand with your feet hip-width apart and place the band around the ball of your left foot.
2. Hold onto a stable object for balance if needed.
3. Slowly raise your left heel off the ground, lifting your body weight onto the ball of your foot.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your left heel back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch to the right leg.

## Pro tips

- Place the band under the ball of your foot and hold the other end for resistance as you rise up on your toes.
- Pause for 2 seconds at the top of each rep to maximize the calf contraction.
- Lower your heel slowly below the starting level for a full stretch before the next rep.

## Common mistakes

- Bouncing at the bottom of the rep instead of pausing in the stretched position.
- Not rising high enough on the toes to achieve a full calf contraction.
- Rushing through reps without pausing at the top or controlling the descent.

## FAQ

### Why train calves one leg at a time?

Single leg work corrects imbalances between legs, doubles the load per calf compared to bilateral work, and improves balance and ankle stability.

### How many reps should you do for single leg calf raises?

Calves respond well to high reps. Aim for 15-25 reps per leg with a 2-second pause at the top and controlled 2-second lowering.

### Can band resistance replace weighted calf raises?

Bands work for hypertrophy if the resistance is sufficient. However, calves often need heavy loading that may exceed what most bands provide.

### What muscles does the Band Single Leg Calf Raise work?

The Band Single Leg Calf Raise primarily targets the calves, with secondary activation in the ankles, feet. It's classified as a strength exercise for the legs.

### Is the Band Single Leg Calf Raise good for beginners?

The Band Single Leg Calf Raise is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of calves strength with easier variations first.
