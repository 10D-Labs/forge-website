# Band Alternating Bicep Curl - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The band alternating bicep curl is an exercise targeting the bicep using a resistance band. It involves curling one arm at a time while keeping the elbows close to the body, providing resistance throughout the movement.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Biceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Forearms
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Band Alternating Bicep Curl

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold the band with an underhand grip, palms facing up.
2. Keep your elbows close to your sides and slowly curl one arm up towards your shoulder, squeezing your biceps at the top.
3. Lower the arm back down to the starting position and repeat with the other arm.
4. Continue alternating arms for the desired number of repetitions.
