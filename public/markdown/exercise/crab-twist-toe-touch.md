# Crab Twist Toe Touch

## Quick answer

The crab twist toe touch is a bodyweight exercise targeting the abdominal muscles, with additional engagement of the obliques and hip flexors. It involves a dynamic movement pattern that requires coordination and balance, as you alternate reaching your hand to the opposite foot while supporting your body in a crab position. Intermediate core exercise using body weight. Targets abs, also works obliques, hip flexors.

- **Body Part:** Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Abs
- **Secondary Muscles:** Obliques, Hip Flexors
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Start by sitting on the ground with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
2. Place your hands behind you, fingers pointing towards your feet, and lift your hips off the ground.
3. Extend one leg straight out in front of you while simultaneously reaching your opposite hand towards your toes.
4. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.
5. Continue alternating sides for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Keep your hips elevated throughout the entire set; don't let your butt sink back to the ground between reps.
- Reach fully with the opposite hand to the opposite foot, rotating your torso to get a full contraction in the obliques.
- Exhale forcefully as you reach for the toe to help engage the deep core muscles.

## Common mistakes

- Dropping the hips between reps, which eliminates the core and glute engagement that makes this exercise effective.
- Rushing through the movement without fully extending the leg or reaching the hand, reducing range of motion.
- Placing too much weight on the wrists instead of distributing it across the palms, causing wrist discomfort.

## FAQ

### What muscles does the Crab Twist Toe Touch work?

It targets the abs and obliques through the twisting motion, while the crab position activates the glutes, shoulders, and triceps to maintain the elevated posture.

### How do I modify the Crab Twist Toe Touch if it's too hard?

Start by just holding the crab position for 20-30 seconds. Once comfortable, add the Leg Extensions without the twist, then progress to the full movement.

### Is the Crab Twist Toe Touch cardio or strength?

It's primarily a core strength exercise, but performed at higher rep ranges or in a circuit, it elevates your heart rate enough to provide a cardio benefit.

### What muscles does the Crab Twist Toe Touch work?

The Crab Twist Toe Touch primarily targets the abs, with secondary activation in the obliques, hip flexors. It's classified as a strength exercise for the core.

### Is the Crab Twist Toe Touch good for beginners?

The Crab Twist Toe Touch is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of abs strength with easier variations first.
