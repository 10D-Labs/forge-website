# Chest Dip - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The chest dip is a bodyweight exercise performed on parallel bars that primarily targets the pectoral muscles, with secondary emphasis on the tricep and shoulders. It requires significant upper body strength, stability, and control.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Chest
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Chest
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Chest Dip

1. Position yourself on parallel bars with your arms fully extended and your body straight.
2. Lower your body by bending your elbows until your shoulders are below your elbows.
3. Push yourself back up to the starting position by straightening your arms.
4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
