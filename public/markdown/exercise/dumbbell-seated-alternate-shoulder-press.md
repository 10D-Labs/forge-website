# Dumbbell Seated Alternate Shoulder Press

## Quick answer

The dumbbell seated alternate shoulder press is a strength exercise targeting the deltoids, performed while seated and alternating arms for each press. It also engages the tricep and upper back as secondary muscles. Beginner shoulders exercise using dumbbell. Targets shoulders, also works triceps, upper back.

- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Upper Back
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Sit on a bench with your back straight and feet flat on the ground.
2. Hold a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing inwards, and raise them to shoulder height.
3. Press one dumbbell up overhead while keeping the other dumbbell at shoulder height.
4. Lower the raised dumbbell back to shoulder height while simultaneously pressing the other dumbbell up overhead.
5. Continue alternating between arms for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Sit tall with your core braced and avoid leaning side to side as you press each dumbbell overhead.
- Press each dumbbell to full lockout overhead before initiating the press on the other side.
- Keep the non-pressing dumbbell at shoulder height with your elbow at 90 degrees to maintain tension.

## Common mistakes

- Leaning the torso away from the pressing arm to use body momentum rather than strict shoulder strength.
- Pressing the dumbbells forward in front of the face instead of straight up overhead.
- Rushing the alternation and not completing a full range of motion on each side.

## FAQ

### Is alternating shoulder press better than pressing both arms together?

Alternating adds a core stability component and increases time under tension for each shoulder. Pressing both is better for maximal load and overall pressing power.

### Should I use a back support bench?

A bench with a back pad set at 85-90 degrees is recommended. It supports your spine and lets you focus purely on pressing without worrying about spinal stability.

### What weight should beginners start with?

Start with 10-15 lb dumbbells and focus on pressing with full range of motion and good control before increasing weight.

### What muscles does the Dumbbell Seated Alternate Shoulder Press work?

The Dumbbell Seated Alternate Shoulder Press primarily targets the shoulders, with secondary activation in the triceps, upper back. It's classified as a strength exercise for the shoulders.

### Is the Dumbbell Seated Alternate Shoulder Press good for beginners?

Yes, the Dumbbell Seated Alternate Shoulder Press is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building shoulders strength.
