# Dumbbell Over Bench Single Arm Neutral Wrist Curl - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell over bench one arm neutral wrist curl is an isolation exercise targeting the bicep and forearms. It is performed seated, with the arm extended over a bench and the wrist in a neutral position, curling the dumbbell towards the shoulder.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Biceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Forearms
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Over Bench Single Arm Neutral Wrist Curl

1. Sit on a bench with a dumbbell in one hand and your arm extended over the bench, palm facing up.
2. Keep your upper arm stationary and curl the dumbbell towards your shoulder, keeping your wrist in a neutral position.
3. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower the dumbbell back to the starting position.
4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions, then switch arms.
