# Dumbbell Lying Hammer Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell lying hammer press is a chest exercise performed on a bench with dumbbells held in a neutral grip (palms facing each other). It targets the pectorals while also engaging the tricep and shoulders.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Chest
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Chest
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Lying Hammer Press

1. Lie flat on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing each other and arms extended straight up.
2. Lower the dumbbells to the sides of your chest, keeping your elbows at a 90 degree angle.
3. Press the dumbbells back up to the starting position, fully extending your arms.
4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
