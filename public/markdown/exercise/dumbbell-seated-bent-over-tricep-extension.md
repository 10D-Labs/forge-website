# Dumbbell Seated Bent Over Tricep Extension

## Quick answer

The dumbbell seated bent over tricep extension is an isolation exercise targeting the tricep, performed while seated and bent over, extending the arms backward with dumbbells. Intermediate arms exercise using dumbbell. Targets triceps, also works shoulders, back.

- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Shoulders, Back
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Sit on a bench with your feet flat on the ground and hold a dumbbell in each hand.
2. Bend forward at the waist, keeping your back straight and your head up.
3. Extend your arms straight back, keeping your elbows close to your head.
4. Pause for a moment, then slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Bend forward until your chest is close to your thighs, then extend both arms straight back simultaneously.
- Keep your elbows tight to your body and only move at the elbow joint; your upper arms should stay still.
- Squeeze both triceps at full extension and hold for a beat before lowering.

## Common mistakes

- Raising the torso during the extension, which uses momentum instead of tricep contraction.
- Not bending forward enough, which reduces the gravitational challenge on the triceps.
- Swinging the dumbbells with body momentum rather than extending them with controlled muscular force.

## FAQ

### Is this the same as a regular kickback?

Similar, but this version works both arms simultaneously while seated, which provides more core stability and ensures both triceps work equally.

### How heavy should I go?

Light to moderate weight works best. The bent-over position and bilateral arm extension make it challenging with even modest weight. Focus on the squeeze at full extension.

### Does this exercise work all three tricep heads?

It primarily targets the lateral and long heads of the triceps. The bent-over position and full extension emphasize the long head due to the shoulder position.

### What muscles does the Dumbbell Seated Bent Over Tricep Extension work?

The Dumbbell Seated Bent Over Tricep Extension primarily targets the triceps, with secondary activation in the shoulders, back. It's classified as a strength exercise for the arms.

### Is the Dumbbell Seated Bent Over Tricep Extension good for beginners?

The Dumbbell Seated Bent Over Tricep Extension is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of triceps strength with easier variations first.
