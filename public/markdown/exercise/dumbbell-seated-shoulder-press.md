# Dumbbell Seated Shoulder Press - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell seated shoulder press is a strength exercise targeting the deltoid muscles of the shoulders. It is performed while seated, pressing dumbbells overhead to build shoulder and upper arm strength.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Upper Back
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Seated Shoulder Press

1. Sit on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, resting on your thighs.
2. Raise the dumbbells to shoulder height, palms facing forward.
3. Press the dumbbells upward until your arms are fully extended overhead.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
