# Resistance Band Seated Shoulder Press

## Quick answer

The resistance band seated shoulder press is an overhead pressing exercise performed while seated, using a resistance band to target the deltoid muscles. It also engages the tricep and upper back as secondary muscles. This exercise is suitable for building shoulder strength and stability, and is accessible for most fitness levels due to the controlled movement and support provided by the seated position. Beginner shoulders exercise using resistance band. Targets shoulders, also works triceps, upper back.

- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Upper Back
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Sit on a chair or bench with your back straight and feet flat on the ground.
2. Hold the resistance band with both hands, palms facing forward, and bring it up to shoulder level.
3. Press the band overhead, extending your arms fully.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower the band back down to shoulder level.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Sit on the center of the band to anchor it and keep consistent tension throughout the press.
- Press straight up and slightly inward so the hands nearly touch at the top of the movement.
- Control the descent and do not let the band snap your arms back down to the starting position.

## Common mistakes

- Not sitting on enough of the band, resulting in insufficient tension for a training effect.
- Arching the lower back excessively as you press overhead, which strains the lumbar spine.
- Pressing the arms forward instead of straight up, which turns it into a front raise and reduces shoulder press effectiveness.

## FAQ

### Can resistance Band Shoulder Presses build muscle?

Yes, especially for beginners and intermediate lifters. The band provides progressive resistance that challenges the shoulders through the full range of motion.

### What band tension should I use for seated shoulder press?

Medium resistance is a good starting point. You should be able to complete 12-15 reps with good form. If you cannot press the band overhead, use a lighter band.

### Is Band Shoulder Press good for shoulder rehab?

Yes, bands provide accommodating resistance that is gentler on the joints at the start of the movement and progressively challenges the muscles through the range.

### What muscles does the Resistance Band Seated Shoulder Press work?

The Resistance Band Seated Shoulder Press primarily targets the shoulders, with secondary activation in the triceps, upper back. It's classified as a strength exercise for the shoulders.

### Is the Resistance Band Seated Shoulder Press good for beginners?

Yes, the Resistance Band Seated Shoulder Press is rated as a beginner-level exercise. It's a great starting point for building shoulders strength.
