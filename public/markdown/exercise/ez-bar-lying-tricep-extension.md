# EZ Bar Lying Tricep Extension - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The EZ bar lying close grip tricep extension behind head is an isolation exercise targeting the tricep, performed lying on a bench with a close grip on an EZ bar. It requires control and proper form to avoid shoulder strain and maximize tricep engagement.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** EZ Bar
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform EZ Bar Lying Tricep Extension

1. Lie flat on a bench with your feet flat on the ground and your head at the end of the bench.
2. Hold the ez barbell with a close grip, palms facing up, and extend your arms straight up over your chest.
3. Keeping your upper arms stationary, slowly lower the barbell behind your head by bending your elbows.
4. Pause for a moment, then extend your arms back up to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
