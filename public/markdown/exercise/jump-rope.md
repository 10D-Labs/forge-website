# Jump Rope - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

Jump rope is a cardiovascular exercise that involves continuously jumping over a swinging rope. It primarily targets the cardiovascular system while also engaging the calves, quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Cardio
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Cardiovascular
- **Secondary Muscles:** Calves, Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Cardio

---

## How to Perform Jump Rope

1. Hold the handles of the jump rope with your hands, palms facing inward.
2. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and knees slightly bent.
3. Swing the rope over your head and jump over it as it comes towards your feet.
4. Land softly on the balls of your feet and repeat the jump as the rope comes around again.
5. Continue jumping for the desired duration or number of repetitions.
