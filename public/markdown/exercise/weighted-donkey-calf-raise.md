# Weighted Donkey Calf Raise - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The weighted donkey calf raise is a calf-focused exercise performed on a raised platform, where the lifter raises and lowers their heels while holding additional weight. This movement targets the calves and also engages the hamstrings and glutes as secondary muscles.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Calves
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Weighted Donkey Calf Raise

1. Stand on a raised platform with your toes on the edge and your heels hanging off.
2. Hold onto a stable object for support.
3. Raise your heels as high as possible by extending your ankles.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your heels back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
