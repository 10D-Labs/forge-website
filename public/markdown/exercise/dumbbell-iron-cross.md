# Dumbbell Iron Cross - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell iron cross is a shoulder exercise that targets the deltoids by raising the arms out to the sides while holding dumbbells, forming a T shape with the body. It also engages the tricep and chest as secondary muscles.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Chest
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Iron Cross

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand at your sides.
2. Raise your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the ground, forming a T shape with your body.
3. Pause for a moment, then slowly lower your arms back down to the starting position.
4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
