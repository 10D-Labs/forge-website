# Cable Lateral Raise - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The cable lateral raise is an isolation exercise that targets the deltoid muscles, particularly the lateral (middle) head. It is performed using a cable machine, which provides constant tension throughout the movement. This exercise helps build shoulder width and strength.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Cable
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Traps, Triceps
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Cable Lateral Raise

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and grasp the cable handles with an overhand grip.
2. Keep your arms straight and your core engaged.
3. Raise your arms out to the sides until they are parallel to the floor.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your arms back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
