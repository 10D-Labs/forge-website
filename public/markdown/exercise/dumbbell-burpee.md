# Dumbbell Burpee

## Quick answer

The dumbbell burpee is a full-body exercise that combines a squat, pushup, and overhead press using dumbbells. It targets the cardiovascular system while also engaging multiple muscle groups, including the legs, shoulders, triceps, and core. Advanced cardio exercise using dumbbell. Targets cardiovascular, also works quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, shoulders, triceps, core.

- **Body Part:** Cardio
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Cardiovascular
- **Secondary Muscles:** Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Calves, Shoulders, Triceps, Core
- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Category:** Cardio

---

## How to perform

1. Start in a standing position with your feet shoulder-width apart and a dumbbell in each hand.
2. Lower your body into a squat position, placing the dumbbells on the ground in front of you.
3. Kick your feet back into a pushup position, keeping your body in a straight line.
4. Perform a pushup, bending your elbows and lowering your chest towards the ground.
5. Jump your feet back towards your hands, landing in a squat position.
6. Stand up explosively, lifting the dumbbells off the ground and bringing them to your shoulders.
7. Press the dumbbells overhead, fully extending your arms.
8. Lower the dumbbells back to your shoulders and repeat the entire sequence for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Use hexagonal dumbbells that will not roll to create a stable base during the Pushup portion.
- Keep the dumbbells directly under your shoulders when in the Pushup position for wrist safety.
- Press the dumbbells overhead at the top with full hip extension for a complete rep.

## Common mistakes

- Using round dumbbells that can roll, which creates a serious wrist injury risk during the Pushup.
- Not cleaning the dumbbells to the shoulders before pressing, which limits the weight you can press overhead.
- Sacrificing form for speed, especially rounding the back during the pickup and letting the hips sag during the Pushup.

## FAQ

### What weight dumbbells should you use for Dumbbell Burpees?

Start with 15-20 pound dumbbells for men and 8-12 pounds for women. The overhead press at the top is the limiting factor, so choose a weight you can press when fatigued.

### Are Dumbbell Burpees better than regular Burpees?

They add an upper body strength component (the row or press) and increase overall workload. They are harder but more comprehensive as a total-body exercise.

### How many Dumbbell Burpees should you do?

Start with 3 sets of 5-8 reps. They are significantly more demanding than regular Burpees, so keep reps low and focus on form.

### What muscles does the Dumbbell Burpee work?

The Dumbbell Burpee primarily targets the cardiovascular, with secondary activation in the quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, shoulders, triceps, core. It's classified as a cardio exercise for the cardio.

### Is the Dumbbell Burpee good for beginners?

The Dumbbell Burpee is an advanced exercise. Beginners should master fundamental cardio movements first.
