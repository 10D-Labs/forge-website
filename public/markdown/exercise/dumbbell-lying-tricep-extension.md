# Dumbbell Lying Tricep Extension - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell lying tricep extension is an isolation exercise targeting the tricep, performed while lying on a bench and extending the arms with dumbbells.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Arms
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Triceps
- **Secondary Muscles:** Shoulders
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Lying Tricep Extension

1. Lie flat on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing each other.
2. Extend your arms straight up over your chest, keeping your elbows close to your body.
3. Lower the dumbbells down towards your forehead, bending your elbows.
4. Pause for a moment, then extend your arms back up to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
