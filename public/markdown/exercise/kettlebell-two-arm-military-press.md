# Kettlebell Two Arm Military Press

## Quick answer

The kettlebell two arm military press is a shoulder exercise that involves pressing two kettlebells overhead from shoulder level, targeting the deltoids and engaging the tricep and upper back. Intermediate shoulders exercise using kettlebell. Targets shoulders, also works triceps, upper back.

- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Kettlebell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Upper Back
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a kettlebell in each hand at shoulder level with your palms facing forward.
2. Engage your core and press the kettlebells overhead, fully extending your arms.
3. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower the kettlebells back to the starting position.
4. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Clean both kettlebells to a solid rack position before pressing. Elbows tight, bells resting on the outer forearms.
- Press both kettlebells straight up simultaneously, finishing with biceps by your ears and wrists stacked over shoulders.
- Squeeze your glutes and brace your abs hard throughout the press to create a stable platform.

## Common mistakes

- Pressing with one arm faster than the other, creating an asymmetrical lockout.
- Leaning back to compensate for heavy weight instead of maintaining a vertical torso.
- Flaring the elbows out to the sides during the press instead of keeping them slightly in front of the body.

## FAQ

### Can I use the double Military Press as my main overhead pressing exercise?

Absolutely. The double kettlebell Military Press is a fantastic primary shoulder builder that also challenges core stability. It's a staple in many strength programs.

### How does the double kettlebell press compare to a barbell press?

Kettlebells allow a more natural pressing groove since each arm moves independently. They also demand more stabilization. However, a barbell allows heavier absolute loads, making it better for pure strength at advanced levels.

### What muscles does the Kettlebell Two Arm Military Press work?

The Kettlebell Two Arm Military Press primarily targets the shoulders, with secondary activation in the triceps, upper back. It's classified as a strength exercise for the shoulders.

### Is the Kettlebell Two Arm Military Press good for beginners?

The Kettlebell Two Arm Military Press is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of shoulders strength with easier variations first.
