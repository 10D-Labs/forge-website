# Bodyweight Squat - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

A bodyweight squat exercise primarily targeting the quadriceps, with secondary activation of the hamstrings and glutes. This movement is fundamental for lower body strength and can be performed without equipment.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Quads
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Bodyweight Squat

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back as if sitting on a chair.
3. Keep your chest up and your back straight.
4. Lower yourself until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
5. Push through your heels to return to the starting position.
6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
