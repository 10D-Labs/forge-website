# Cable Standing Calf Raise - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The cable standing calf raise is an exercise that targets the calf muscles using a cable machine for resistance. It involves standing upright and raising your heels off the ground to engage the calves, with secondary activation of the hamstrings and glutes.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Cable
- **Target Muscle:** Calves
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hamstrings, Glutes
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Cable Standing Calf Raise

1. Stand facing a cable machine with your feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Hold onto the cable machine handles or attach a cable ankle strap to your ankles.
3. Raise your heels off the ground by extending your ankles as high as possible.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your heels back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
