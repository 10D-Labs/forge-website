# Farmers Walk (Time) - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The farmer's walk is a functional strength exercise where you walk while holding a dumbbell in each hand, engaging the quads, calves, forearms, and core. It improves grip strength, core stability, and overall conditioning.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Legs
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Quads
- **Secondary Muscles:** Calves, Forearms, Core
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength
- **Tracking Type:** time

---

## How to Perform Farmers Walk (Time)

1. Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing your sides.
2. Keep your back straight and your shoulders back.
3. Take small, controlled steps forward, maintaining an upright posture.
4. Continue walking for your target duration.
5. To finish, stop walking and carefully lower the dumbbells to your sides.
