# Cross Body Crunch - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The cross body crunch is a bodyweight abdominal exercise that targets the abs and obliques. It involves twisting the torso to bring an elbow towards the opposite knee, engaging the core muscles.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Abs
- **Secondary Muscles:** Obliques
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Cross Body Crunch

1. Lie flat on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground.
2. Place your hands behind your head with your elbows pointing outwards.
3. Engaging your abs, lift your upper body off the ground and twist to bring your right elbow towards your left knee.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your upper body back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat on the other side, bringing your left elbow towards your right knee.
6. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
