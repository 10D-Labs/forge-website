# Band Kneeling Twisting Crunch - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The band kneeling twisting crunch is an abdominal exercise that targets the abs and obliques using a resistance band. It involves kneeling and twisting the torso against the resistance of the band, engaging the core muscles.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Core
- **Equipment:** Resistance Band
- **Target Muscle:** Abs
- **Secondary Muscles:** Obliques
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Band Kneeling Twisting Crunch

1. Attach the band to a sturdy anchor point at waist height.
2. Kneel down facing away from the anchor point and hold the band with both hands, keeping your elbows bent and close to your sides.
3. Engage your abs and slowly twist your torso to one side, bringing your hands towards your opposite hip.
4. Pause for a moment, then slowly return to the starting position.
5. Repeat on the other side.
6. Continue alternating sides for the desired number of repetitions.
