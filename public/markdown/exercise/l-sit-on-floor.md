# L-sit on Floor - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The L-sit on floor is a challenging core exercise that targets the abdominal muscles and hip flexors. It requires significant core strength, balance, and coordination to lift and hold the legs parallel to the floor while supporting the body with the arms.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Core
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Abs
- **Secondary Muscles:** Hip Flexors
- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform L-sit on Floor

1. Sit on the floor with your legs extended in front of you.
2. Place your hands on the floor beside your hips, fingers pointing forward.
3. Engage your core and lift your legs off the ground, keeping them straight.
4. Try to bring your legs parallel to the floor, forming an 'L' shape with your body.
5. Hold this position for as long as you can.
6. Slowly lower your legs back down to the starting position.
7. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
