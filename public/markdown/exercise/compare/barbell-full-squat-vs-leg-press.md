# Barbell Full Squat vs Leg Press

## Quick answer

Barbell Full Squat targets the glutes using barbell (Intermediate), while Leg Press targets the glutes using machine (Beginner).

---

| Equipment | Barbell | Machine |
| Difficulty | Intermediate | Beginner |
| Category | Strength | Strength |

## FAQ

### What's the difference between Barbell Full Squat and Leg Press?

Barbell Full Squat targets the glutes using barbell, while Leg Press targets the glutes using machine.

### Which is better for beginners?

Leg Press is better for beginners.

### Can I do both in the same workout?

Yes, you can include both exercises in your routine since they both target legs. Alternate between them to work your muscles from different angles.
