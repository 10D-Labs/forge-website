# Barbell Close Grip Bench Press vs Tricep Dip

## Quick answer

Barbell Close Grip Bench Press targets the triceps using barbell (Intermediate), while Tricep Dip targets the triceps using body weight (Beginner).

## FAQ

### What's the difference between Barbell Close Grip Bench Press and Tricep Dip?

Barbell Close Grip Bench Press targets the triceps using barbell, while Tricep Dip targets the triceps using body weight.

### Which is better for beginners?

Tricep Dip is better for beginners.

### Can I do both in the same workout?

Yes, you can include both exercises in your routine since they both target arms. Alternate between them to work your muscles from different angles.
