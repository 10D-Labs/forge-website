# Barbell Bench Press vs Dumbbell Bench Press

## Quick answer

Barbell Bench Press targets the chest using barbell (Intermediate), while Dumbbell Bench Press targets the chest using dumbbell (Beginner).

---

## Comparison

| Attribute | Barbell Bench Press | Dumbbell Bench Press |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Body Part | Chest | Chest |
| Target | Chest | Chest |
| Equipment | Barbell | Dumbbell |
| Difficulty | Intermediate | Beginner |
| Category | Strength | Strength |
| Secondary | Triceps, Shoulders | Triceps, Shoulders |

## FAQ

### What's the difference between Barbell Bench Press and Dumbbell Bench Press?

Barbell Bench Press targets the chest using barbell, while Dumbbell Bench Press targets the chest using dumbbell.

### Which is better for beginners?

Dumbbell Bench Press is better for beginners.

### Can I do both in the same workout?

Yes, you can include both exercises in your routine since they both target chest. Alternate between them to work your muscles from different angles.
