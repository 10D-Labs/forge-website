# Weighted Kneeling Step with Swing

## Quick answer

The weighted kneeling step with swing is a dynamic exercise that targets the deltoids while also engaging the tricep and core. It involves swinging weights overhead from a kneeling position, requiring coordination, balance, and strength. Intermediate shoulders exercise using body weight. Targets shoulders, also works triceps, core.

- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Body Weight
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Triceps, Core
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to perform

1. Start in a kneeling position with your knees hip-width apart and your back straight.
2. Hold a weight in each hand, with your arms extended straight down in front of you.
3. Engage your core and swing the weights up and overhead, keeping your arms straight.
4. Lower the weights back down to the starting position and repeat for the desired number of repetitions.

## Pro tips

- Generate the swing from your hips and core, not from your arms pulling the weight up.
- Keep your spine neutral throughout; avoid arching your back at the top of the swing.
- Start with a light weight to learn the hip hinge and arm swing coordination before adding load.

## Common mistakes

- Using the arms to lift the weight instead of generating power from the hip extension and core.
- Hyperextending the lower back at the top of the swing rather than stopping with arms overhead and core braced.
- Letting the weight pull you forward on the downswing because of inadequate core bracing.

## FAQ

### What muscles does this exercise target?

Primarily the shoulders and core, with significant contribution from the glutes and hip flexors during the step and swing phases.

### What kind of weight should I use?

A kettlebell or single dumbbell works best. Start with 10-15 lbs and increase once you can control the swing and step mechanics cleanly.

### Is this good for conditioning?

Yes, the combination of kneeling, stepping, and overhead swinging elevates the heart rate significantly while building full-body coordination.

### What muscles does the Weighted Kneeling Step with Swing work?

The Weighted Kneeling Step with Swing primarily targets the shoulders, with secondary activation in the triceps, core. It's classified as a strength exercise for the shoulders.

### Is the Weighted Kneeling Step with Swing good for beginners?

The Weighted Kneeling Step with Swing is rated as intermediate. Beginners should build a base of shoulders strength with easier variations first.
