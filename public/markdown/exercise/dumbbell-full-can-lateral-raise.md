# Dumbbell Full Can Lateral Raise - Forge Exercise Library

## Quick Answer

The dumbbell full can lateral raise is a shoulder exercise that targets the deltoids and also engages the traps and rotator cuff. It involves raising dumbbells to the sides with a slight bend in the elbows, mimicking the motion of pouring from a can, which helps reduce shoulder impingement risk.

**Details:**
- **Body Part:** Shoulders
- **Equipment:** Dumbbell
- **Target Muscle:** Shoulders
- **Secondary Muscles:** Traps, Rotator Cuff
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Category:** Strength

---

## How to Perform Dumbbell Full Can Lateral Raise

1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing your body.
2. Keep your back straight and engage your core.
3. Raise your arms out to the sides, keeping a slight bend in your elbows, until they are parallel to the ground.
4. Pause for a moment at the top, then slowly lower your arms back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
