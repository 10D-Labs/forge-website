## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Forge subscription at any time through your device settings:

**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Forge**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

**Android:**
1. Open the **Google Play Store**
2. Tap your profile icon
3. Tap **Payments & subscriptions** > **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Forge**
5. Tap **Cancel subscription**

Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

---

### How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data through the app or by email. See our [Account Deletion](/delete-account) page for full instructions.

---

### How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

1. Download Forge on your new device
2. Sign in with the same account you used to purchase
3. Your subscription will automatically restore

If it doesn't restore automatically, go to **Settings** in the app and tap **Restore Purchases**.

---

### The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps:

1. **Force close and reopen** the app
2. **Check for updates** in the App Store or Google Play
3. **Restart your device**
4. **Check your internet connection**

If the issue persists, email us at [support@forgetrainer.ai](mailto:support@forgetrainer.ai) with:
- Your device model
- Your operating system version
- A description of the issue
- Screenshots if possible

---

### How do I change my email or password?

1. Open the Forge app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Tap **Account**
4. Update your email or password

---

### Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google, depending on where you purchased your subscription:

- **Apple:** [Request a refund from Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084)
- **Google Play:** [Request a refund from Google](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637)
